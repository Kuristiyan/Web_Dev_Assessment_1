Recipe: Simple Chocolate Truffle

A rich, melt-in-your-mouth treat that combines condensed milk and cocoa powder.
They offer a smooth, creamy, and decadent bite-sized chocolate experience that is easy to make with only few ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Optional: Chocolate Truffle Coating (i.e. Sprinkles, crushed nuts, shredded coconut, pulverized peppermints etc.)

Methods:

  1. In a pot, heat the condensed milk over medium-low heat until it becomes loose and tiny bubbles start to appear around the edges.
  2. Move the heated condensed milk to a mixing bowl to make mixing easier and avoid boiling.
  3. Gradually add the cocoa powder to the condensed milk, stirring continuously until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. Once the mixture is smooth, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until the chocolate blend is firm and maintains its shape.
  5. After the chocolate blend has set, use a spoon to scoop out small portions of the mixture and roll them into balls.
  6. Roll the chocolate balls in your choice of coating, such as chocolate sprinkles, Oreos, or shredded coconut.
