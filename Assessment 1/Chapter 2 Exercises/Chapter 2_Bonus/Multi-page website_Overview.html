TONIKAWA: Over The Moon For You

This a the story of Nasa Yuzaki, a boy who falls in love at first sight with the enigmatic Tsukasa. After confessing his feelings, Tsukasa agrees to date him, but only on the condition that they get married.
Thus begins their heartwarming and comedic journey as a newlywed couple navigating life and love together.
